## 2. Legislative Effectiveness (Ranking de Parlamentares)

### Artigo/Livro Principal

- **Autores**: Craig Volden e Alan E. Wiseman
- **Titulo**: Legislative Effectiveness in the United States Congress: The Lawmakers
- **Editora**: Cambridge University Press
- **Ano**: 2014

### Metodologia do LES (Legislative Effectiveness Score):

- Rastreia bills introduzidas por legisladores
- Acompanha progressao: introducao > comite > votacao > lei
- Pondera por significancia (commemorative, substantive, significant)
- Fatores: seniority, majority party, committee chair

### Outros trabalhos dos autores:

- "The Legislative Effectiveness of Women in Congress" (2010)
- Senate LES (1973-2015)
- Center for Effective Lawmaking (CEL) - thelawmakers.org
